# Code of Conduct

This project is a small, friendly open-source kit. The rule is simple: **be respectful.**

## What we expect

- Be kind and assume good intent. People come here with different backgrounds and experience levels.
- Give feedback on the work, not the person. Critique ideas, not people.
- Respect privacy. The whole point of this kit is context about people and companies — never ask anyone to share sensitive data, and don't paste other people's private information into issues, PRs or discussions.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind.

## Scope

This applies everywhere the project lives: issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other channel connected to the repository.

## Reporting

If something isn't right, email **mikolaj.brunka@nocodework.io**. Reports are handled privately. Maintainers may edit, remove, or block content and contributors that break these rules.
